# Account Types

## Mehmet Flex Checking
- Monthly fee: $0 with $500 minimum daily balance, otherwise $8/month
- Transactions: Unlimited free
- ATM: Free at 55,000+ Allpoint ATMs; $2.50 fee at out-of-network ATMs
- Debit card: Included at no charge
- Overdraft protection: Available via linked Mehmet Grow Savings
- Minimum opening deposit: $25

## Mehmet Grow Savings
- APY: 4.50% (current as of Q2 2026)
- Monthly fee: $0 with $100 minimum balance or $50/month recurring deposit
- Withdrawals: Up to 6 free per month; $5 fee per transaction after that
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- FDIC insured up to $250,000

## Mehmet Fixed Term Deposit (CD)
- Available terms: 3, 6, 12, 24, 36 months
- Rates:
  - 3 months: 4.20% APY
  - 6 months: 4.60% APY
  - 12 months: 4.85% APY
  - 24 months: 4.75% APY
  - 36 months: 4.65% APY
- Minimum deposit: $1,000
- Early withdrawal penalty: 90 days of interest

## Account Limits
- Daily ATM withdrawal: $500 (standard), $1,000 (premium tier)
- Daily debit purchase: $2,500 (standard), $5,000 (premium tier)
- Daily internal transfer: $10,000
- Daily wire transfer: $50,000 (requires phone verification)
- Zelle send limit: $2,500/day, $10,000/month
