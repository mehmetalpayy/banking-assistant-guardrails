# Credit Cards

## Mehmet Classic Card
- Annual fee: $0
- Purchase APR: 19.99%–26.99% variable
- Cash advance APR: 27.99%
- Credit limit: $500–$5,000
- Rewards: 1% cash back on all purchases
- Foreign transaction fee: 3%
- Grace period: 25 days
- Best for: Building or rebuilding credit

## Mehmet Gold Card
- Annual fee: $95
- Purchase APR: 17.99%–24.99% variable
- Cash advance APR: 26.99%
- Credit limit: $5,000–$20,000
- Rewards: 2% cash back on groceries and dining; 1.5% on all other purchases
- Foreign transaction fee: None
- Travel insurance: Included (trip cancellation, lost luggage)
- Grace period: 25 days
- Best for: Everyday spending and moderate travelers

## Mehmet Platinum Card
- Annual fee: $250
- Purchase APR: 15.99%–22.99% variable
- Cash advance APR: 25.99%
- Credit limit: $20,000–$50,000
- Rewards: 3% cash back on travel; 2% on dining; 1% on everything else
- Foreign transaction fee: None
- Airport lounge access: 2 complimentary visits per year
- Concierge service: 24/7 dedicated line
- Grace period: 25 days
- Best for: Frequent travelers and high spenders

## General Policies
- Minimum payment: $25 or 2% of outstanding balance, whichever is greater
- Late payment fee: $39
- Over-limit fee: $29
- Balance transfer fee: 3% of amount transferred (minimum $5)
- Credit limit increase: Available after 6 consecutive on-time payments
- Cash advance fee: $10 or 5% of amount, whichever is greater
